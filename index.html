Speech Language Pathologist

Working with and helping out children has always been a dream of mine. Throughout my school years I have volunteered at different schools. With my volunteer work, I have been able to communicate with young children and assist them with their work. Aside from helping children out, I have also had various opportunities to see how they behave in different environments (classroom, playground, etc.). Overall, I have made sure to work with children to see what my career will consist of.

Not only have I volunteered at schools, but also at my local hospital. The field I am interested in works with people of all ages. Aside from working with children, I would also like to work with older people. My volunteer work at my local hospital has allowed me to improve my communication skills, as well as my customer service skills, which involves understanding an individual and making sure that they are treated well.

Overall, my main goal is to help individuals improve their communication skills and make sure they have everything they need to reach their goals.
